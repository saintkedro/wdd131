Uyo Smash Club Site Plan

WDD 131 - Final Project Planning

Site Name

Name: Uyo Smash Club

Reason: I chose this name to combine regional identity ("Uyo") with the active, competitive spirit of tennis ("Smash"). It is professional and locally recognizable.

Site Purpose

The Uyo Smash Club website serves as a community hub for lawn tennis enthusiasts in Uyo. It helps beginners find coaching (The Academy), tracks player standings (The Ladder), and manages membership registration.

Scenarios

  1. As a parent in Uyo, where can I find the list of certified coaches and beginner lesson times?
  2. I just won a match; how do I check my updated position on the competitive leaderboard?

Color Scheme

Primary: Grass Green (#2D5A27)
Secondary: Clay Orange (#E07A5F)
Accent: Tennis Yellow (#F4D35E)

Usage: Green for navigation/headers, Orange for buttons/borders, and Yellow for highlights/badges.

Typography

Heading Font: "Trebuchet MS" (sans-serif) - Page titles and headers.

Body Font: Arial (sans-serif) - Paragraphs, lists, and form labels.
